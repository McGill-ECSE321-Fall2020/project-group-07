The Online Gallery System shall be able to process user requests in less than 5 seconds, 80% of the time. 
(i.e. display artwork from a user's search)

The Online Gallery System shall allow the artist to upload artwork to their profile at any time.

The Online Gallery System shall allow the artist to set a price for each of their pieces.

The Online Gallery System shall remove artwork from display after its purchase by a customer.

The Online Gallery System shall allow users to log in to their registered profiles.
