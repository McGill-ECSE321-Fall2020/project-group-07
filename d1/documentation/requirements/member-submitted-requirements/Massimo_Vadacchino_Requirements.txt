R1. Functional – Upon the registration of a profile, the Online Gallery System shall allow the artist to edit their profile at any time. 
R2. Functional – Upon the creation of an order, the Online Gallery System shall allow the customer to cancel their order 10 minutes at any time.
R3. Functional – On the last working day of each month, a summary of the number of art pieces sold, their costs, and the most visited artists shall be generated by the Online Gallery System.
R4. Non-Functional (Reliability) – The Online Gallery System shall be unavailable no more than once a month.
